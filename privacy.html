Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 25, 2025
Last Updated: June 25, 2025

1. Introduction

Orkestrix ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use Orkestrix.

Privacy First: Orkestrix processes all container data locally on your Mac. We do not upload your container contents, images, or configurations to cloud servers.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Local Data Processing

Orkestrix processes the following data locally on your Mac:

  • Container configurations and metadata
  • Image information and registry credentials
  • Application logs and monitoring data
  • User preferences and settings

2.2 Subscription Information

Through Apple's App Store, we receive:

  • Subscription status and payment verification
  • App Store account identifier (not personal information)
  • Purchase and renewal dates

3. How We Use Information

3.1 Core Functionality

  • Managing and monitoring containers on your Mac
  • Storing user preferences and settings
  • Providing real-time container status and logs

3.2 Service Improvement

  • Verifying subscription status through App Store
  • Improving app performance and reliability
  • Developing new features based on usage patterns

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

  • All container data remains on your Mac
  • Application data stored in macOS application support directories
  • Credentials stored securely in macOS Keychain when possible

4.2 No Cloud Storage

Important: Orkestrix does not upload your container data to cloud servers. No remote storage of container contents, images, or configurations. All processing happens locally on your Mac.

4.3 Security Measures

  • Data encrypted using macOS standard encryption
  • Secure credential storage via Keychain Services
  • Regular security updates through App Store

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Apple App Store

  • Subscription management handled by Apple
  • Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy
  • We receive limited subscription status information only

5.2 Container Registries

  • Direct connections to Docker Hub, GitHub Container Registry, etc.
  • Your registry credentials stored locally in Keychain
  • We do not intercept or store registry communications

6. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties except:

  • As required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights or safety
  • With your explicit consent

7. Children's Privacy

Orkestrix is designed for professional developers and IT professionals. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. International Users

Orkestrix processes data locally on your Mac regardless of your location. No data is transmitted to international servers.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access data stored by Orkestrix on your Mac
  • Delete application data by uninstalling the app
  • Opt out of analytics collection in app settings
  • Cancel your subscription through App Store settings

10. Data Retention

  • Local application data retained until app is uninstalled
  • Subscription data retained by Apple per their policies
  • Analytics data (if enabled) retained for 12 months maximum

11. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect:

  • Changes in app functionality
  • Legal or regulatory requirements
  • Industry best practices

Significant changes will be communicated through:

  • App Store update notes
  • In-app notifications
  • Email if we have your contact information

12. Compliance

Orkestrix is designed to comply with:

  • Apple's App Store privacy requirements
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) where applicable
